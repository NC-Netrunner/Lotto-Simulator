## Lotto Simulator

According to people smarter than me, your chances of winning a Lottery jackpot are roughly the same as your chances of coin flipping 28 heads in a row.
This simple web app is designed to visualise how long that may or may not take.
When the background worker is engaged, it will run approximately 1000 coin flips per second and calculate this into real time, at a rate of approximately 10 flips per real-world second.
